🔒 Privacy Policy – Kouba App

Last Updated: June 12, 2026 | Effective Date: July 12, 2026

Kouba respects your privacy and complies with Moroccan Law No. 09-08 on the protection of individuals with regard to processing of personal data, and Law No. 53-05 on electronic exchange of legal data.

📌 Data Controller: Mohamed Mustafa Lamjahdi (Auto‑entrepreneur), registered address: Laayoune, Kingdom of Morocco. Email: admin@kouba.site.

1. Information We Collect

A. Basic Account Info (All Users)

  • Full name, email address, mobile phone number, city/address, profile picture (optional).

B. Driver-Specific Data

  • CIN, driver's license, vehicle ownership/tanker photos, insurance, health/inspection certificates, default water source (Ayaafa/TanTan), current tank capacity (updated every 12 hours).

C. Location & Usage

  • GPS location (only when app is active for customers; for drivers during active trips), device info, app version, order details, ratings, transaction history.

D. Payment Info

  • Digital wallet balance, transaction history, voucher codes. We do not store credit card numbers – payments are processed through secure third-party gateways.

2. Legal Bases for Processing (Law 09-08 & GDPR)

  • Contract performance: To provide delivery services, process payments, and manage your account.
  • Legitimate interests: To improve the app, prevent fraud, and ensure security.
  • Consent: For marketing communications and personalised advertising (you may withdraw anytime).
  • Legal obligation: To comply with Moroccan laws and respond to judicial requests.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core services (matching, tracking, payments).
  • Improve the app, fix bugs, analyze usage.
  • Send order notifications, updates, support replies, and (with consent) marketing communications.
  • Verify driver identity, prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations (CNDP, judicial orders).
  • Prepare aggregated statistics (non-personal).
  • Operate rating and reputation system.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your information. Limited sharing occurs only for:

  • Between customer and driver – necessary to complete a trip.
  • Technical providers (SDKs):
    • Firebase (Google) – authentication, realtime database, storage, analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
    • Google Maps API – location and routing. Google Privacy Policy
    • Google Cloud Functions – backend logic.
    • Push notification services (FCM, APNs).
  • Legal/judicial authorities – when required by Moroccan law.
  • With your explicit consent – e.g., for marketing or research.

5. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed on servers located outside Morocco (European data centers operated by Google Cloud Platform). These transfers are protected by standard contractual clauses (SCCs) that ensure an adequate level of data protection.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to remember preferences, analyze usage, and improve security. For full details, see our Cookies Policy.

7. Advertising & Marketing Consent

When you first sign up, we will ask for your separate consent to:

  • Send you promotional notifications (special offers, new features).
  • Use anonymized data to show relevant in-app advertisements.
  • Share non-sensitive app usage data with advertising partners (only if you opt in).

You can change your mind at any time in the app settings or by contacting us. See the Advertising Consent page for more details.

8. Data Storage and Security

  • Data stored on Google Cloud Platform (Firebase) in European data centers (GDPR-compliant).
  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 for transit, encryption at rest.
  • Retention: active account data kept; after deletion, personal data removed within 30 days.
  • In case of a data breach, we notify affected users and CNDP within 72 hours.

9. Automated Decision-Making

Our matching algorithm uses geographic, capacity, and water source filters to suggest drivers. This is not fully automated decision-making that produces legal effects concerning you. You may always contact support for human intervention.

10. Your Rights (Law 09-08)

  • Access, rectification, erasure ("right to be forgotten"), objection to direct marketing, data portability, restriction of processing.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: You may file a complaint with the Moroccan data protection authority (CNDP) at www.cndp.ma or by mail to Rabat.
  • To exercise your rights, email admin@kouba.site. We will respond within 30 days.

11. Children's Privacy

Kouba is not intended for users under 18 without parental consent. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a minor without consent, we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be notified via email or in-app notice.

13. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by Moroccan law. Disputes shall first be resolved amicably; otherwise the competent courts of Laayoune have jurisdiction.
