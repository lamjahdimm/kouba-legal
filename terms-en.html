4. Account Types and Permissions

  • Accept or reject requests within 3 minutes; excessive rejections may affect account status.

6. Pricing and Payments

  • Platform commission per successful delivery: 5 Moroccan Dirhams (5 MAD), deducted from driver's wallet.

  • All prices in MAD, inclusive of taxes.
  • Commission is non-refundable after delivery completion.
  • If customer cancels after driver acceptance but before driver arrival, commission is refunded to driver.
