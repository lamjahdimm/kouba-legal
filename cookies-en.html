🍪 Cookies Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026 | Effective Date: July 12, 2026

This Cookies Policy explains how Kouba uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you use our mobile application and website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (phone, tablet, or computer) when you use an app or visit a website. They help the app remember your actions and preferences over time.

How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies: Required for the app to function (e.g., keeping you logged in, maintaining secure sessions). These cannot be disabled. Retention: session only (deleted when you close the app).
  • Preference cookies: Remember your language choice, map display preferences, and notification settings. Retention: up to 1 year.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the app (e.g., which screens are visited most, crash reports). We use Firebase Analytics and Google Analytics for Firebase. Retention: up to 14 months.
  • Marketing cookies: Used only if you have given explicit consent via the Advertising Consent page. They help us deliver relevant offers and measure campaign effectiveness. Retention: up to 6 months.

Third-party cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Firebase / Analytics – Google's cookie policy | Opt-out of Google Analytics
  • Google Maps – Google's cookie policy
  • Cloudflare (CDN) – Cloudflare cookie policy

Managing cookies in the app

You can control cookies through your device settings:

  • On iOS: Go to Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track". Also, you can enable "Limit Ad Tracking".
  • On Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID or opt out of ad personalization.

Disabling non-essential cookies may not break core functionality but may affect personalized features (e.g., saved language preferences).

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available in the app and on our website.
